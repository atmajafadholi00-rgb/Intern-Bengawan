LiDAR UAV System

Next generation aerial mapping technology combining LiDAR sensors with autonomous UAV platforms to produce high‑precision 3D terrain data.

Innovation Overview

The proposed system integrates lightweight LiDAR sensors with UAV platforms to enable high resolution terrain mapping, vegetation analysis, and infrastructure inspection. Compared to traditional photogrammetry, LiDAR can capture elevation data even through vegetation, enabling accurate digital elevation models.

System Features

High Precision

Centimeter‑level terrain elevation accuracy using LiDAR ranging technology.

Autonomous Flight

Pre‑planned waypoint missions allow automated data collection.

3D Point Cloud

Generates dense 3D point clouds for terrain and structure modeling.

Rapid Survey

Large mapping areas can be surveyed much faster than ground methods.

Operational Workflow

1. Mission Planning
Define survey boundary and altitude using mission planning software.
2. UAV Deployment
UAV equipped with LiDAR sensor performs autonomous flight path.
3. Data Acquisition
LiDAR continuously measures ground distance generating raw point data.
4. Data Processing
Point cloud processed into DEM, DSM, and 3D terrain models.

Applications & Impact

Disaster Mapping

Rapid terrain assessment for landslides and floods.

Forestry Monitoring

Measure canopy height and biomass estimation.

Urban Planning

High accuracy digital elevation models for city development.

Infrastructure Inspection

Mapping power lines, roads, and construction areas.
